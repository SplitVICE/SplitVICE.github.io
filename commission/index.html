Requesting a commission

First of all, thank you for being interested in commissioning me.
At this moment I do art and software commissions. At the Commission types section you can check the status of the commissions.
To commission me you'd have to go through two steps: reading the terms of service document and to fill a commission form.
If you have any kind of question, you can send me a message.

Terms of service

I recommend you to read the terms of service document. In this document you will find:

  • My reserved rights.
  • Your rights and duties.
  • Information about the final product.
  • Payment information.
  • How to procedure on X situations.

Commission form

The commission form is used to gather most of the information I need to start organizing and work on your commission order. Once I've read your form we will have a conversation to gather final feedback.
